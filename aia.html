Goals Create a profile page displaying reviews from real estate review websites.

Buyers

  • Want a mobile-friendly experience that delivers the broker's information and can be quickly referenced from anywhere.
  • Create a list-based carousel component to cycle endlessly through a datastore containing reviews by way of List prototype methods.
